# Market Research Prompts

## Purpose
Focus on actionable insights that directly inform product implementation and differentiation in the competitive landscape.

## Prompts

### Competitor Insights
- "What are the latest feature updates from Replit, CodeSandbox, and similar platforms?"
- "What are users saying about these platforms on social media and review sites?"
- "Identify the top 3 differentiators for Replit and CodeSandbox."

### User Needs
- "What are the most requested features in the collaborative coding space?"
- "What pain points do users face when using existing platforms?"
- "What workflows are underserved by current solutions?"

### Market Trends
- "What are the emerging trends in collaborative coding and developer tools?"
- "How is AI being integrated into developer workflows?"
- "What are the growth drivers for the developer tools market?"

## Advanced Research Tactics
- Use tools like ChatGPT, Bard, or Claude for quick insights.
- Cross-reference findings with data from Statista, Gartner, and industry reports.
- Monitor competitor blogs and forums for updates and user discussions.

## Next Steps
1. Use these prompts to gather insights weekly.
2. Document findings in a shared dashboard for the team.
3. Align insights with the product roadmap to prioritize impactful features.

# Market Analysis

## Purpose
Understand the competitive landscape and opportunity for LaunchKit AI.

## Competitors

1. **Teachable**
   - Strengths: Established platform, robust features.
   - Weaknesses: High fees, limited customization.

2. **Kajabi**
   - Strengths: All-in-one solution.
   - Weaknesses: Expensive, steep learning curve.

3. **Gumroad**
   - Strengths: Simple, creator-friendly.
   - Weaknesses: Limited course-specific features.

## Opportunity
- Focus on AI-driven course creation to differentiate.
- Target creators who value speed and simplicity.
- Offer competitive pricing to undercut premium platforms.

## Next Steps
- Conduct SWOT analysis for each competitor.
- Identify underserved niches in the market.

# User Interviews

## Purpose
Gather direct feedback from target users to validate pain points and refine product direction.

## Interview Questions

1. **Content Creation Challenges**
   - What is your biggest challenge when creating courses?
   - How much time do you spend on content creation?

2. **Technical Barriers**
   - Do you struggle with setting up landing pages or payment systems?
   - What tools do you currently use, and what do you dislike about them?

3. **Monetization Goals**
   - What are your revenue goals for your courses?
   - What prevents you from achieving these goals?

## Next Steps
- Schedule interviews with 5-10 target users.
- Document key insights and quotes.
- Use findings to refine user stories and UX flows.

# User Pain Points

## Purpose
Document the primary pain points faced by target users that LaunchKit AI aims to solve.

## Pain Points

1. **Time-Intensive Content Creation**
   - Problem: Content creators spend 40+ hours turning expertise into sellable courses.
   - Impact: Limits scalability and revenue potential.

2. **Lack of Technical Expertise**
   - Problem: Many creators struggle with building landing pages, payment systems, and course delivery platforms.
   - Impact: High barrier to entry for monetization.

3. **Low Conversion Rates**
   - Problem: Existing tools fail to optimize for user engagement and sales.
   - Impact: Creators struggle to turn visitors into paying customers.

## Validation Plan
- Conduct user interviews to confirm these pain points.
- Analyze competitor tools to identify gaps.
- Survey target audience for additional insights.
